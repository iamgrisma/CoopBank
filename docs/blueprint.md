# **App Name**: CoopBank

## Core Features:

- Member Profiles: Manage member details, KYC, nominee information, and shareholder status.
- Non-Member Client Profiles: Create profiles exclusively for loan-taking with KYC and personal details.
- Share Allotment: Issue and manage share units to new members.
- Share Register: Maintain a digital register of shareholders, Kitta numbers, and share value.
- Share Transactions: Handle share transfers, refunds, and calls for additional capital.
- Dividend Management: Calculate and distribute dividends as cash payouts or transfers to savings accounts.
- Dynamic Savings Scheme Creation: Admin creates savings schemes with configurable parameters like interest rate and withdrawal rules.
- Fixed Deposit Management: Handle fixed deposits with maturity dates, interest payout options, and premature withdrawal penalties.
- Savings Transactions: Enable deposit, withdrawal, and statement generation for savings accounts, with passbook printing utility.
- Dynamic Loan Scheme Creation: Admin creates loan products with configurable parameters like interest rate, repayment tenure, and collateral types.
- Loan Application & Approval Workflow: Manage loan applications, documentation uploads, guarantor management, and a multi-step approval process.
- Disbursement & Repayment: Loan disbursement after approval, generate repayment schedules, collect installments, and handle advance payments.
- Collateral Management: Track collateral linked to loans, including valuation and legal status.
- Savings Interest Calculation: Calculate savings interest based on configurable methods and posting frequency.
- Loan Interest Calculation: Calculates interest based on the loan scheme's defined method.
- Interest Rebate Calculation: Calculate and apply interest rebates for members who pay loan installments on time.
- Penalty Calculation: Automatically calculate and apply penalties for late loan repayments.
- Chart of Accounts: A predefined, but customizable, COA compliant with Nepali cooperative standards.
- Automated Vouchers: All transactions from share, savings, and loan modules automatically generate corresponding journal vouchers.
- Manual Vouchers: A screen for accountants to pass manual journal vouchers for expenses, income, and adjustments.
- Daybook / Cashbook: Real-time tracking of cash-in-hand and bank balances.
- Financial Statements: Real-time generation of trial balance, profit & loss account, and balance sheet.
- Bank Reconciliation: A utility to reconcile system bank balances with actual bank statements.
- Member/Client Reports: Complete member/non-member lists and individual ledgers for shares, savings, and loans.
- Transaction Reports: Daily deposit/withdrawal and loan disbursement/repayment reports.
- Loan Reports: Loan aging report, loan outstanding report, and loan loss provisioning report.
- Compliance Reports: Generate reports required by the local Cooperative Registrar's office and PEARLS monitoring system.
- Financial Reports: All standard financial statements exportable to PDF/Excel.
- Calendar: Operate natively on the Bikram Sambat (B.S.) calendar.
- Language: Bilingual Interface (Nepali & English).
- User Roles & Permissions: Granular, role-based access control for different user types.
